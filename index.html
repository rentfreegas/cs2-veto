Bug found & fixed – 19 Jul 2026, 09:00. Until today the model assumed every BO3 veto used one turn order for the last two bans (ban, ban, pick, pick, then A bans, B bans). Majors, RMRs and PGL events actually flip those final two bans – the team that picked second bans again straight away. About 1 match in 6 overall (and nearly all Major/PGL matches) follow that flipped order. Before: at those events this page named the wrong team on bans 5–6 and computed decider odds as if the wrong team were choosing (typically a few percentage points off). Now: the new "Ban order" switch below lets you pick the convention, and the daily logged predictions detect each event's real order automatically. Unchanged: we replayed 435 affected past matches both ways – the model's top calls (likely decider, first bans, first picks) came out essentially identical, so the graded track record further down stands as-is.
